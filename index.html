About League of Legends

League of Legends is all about teamplay, understanding the game and being able to make strategies according to the players you are going up against.

To understand how league works, one must first know where it is. The location is called Summoners Rift.




There are 11 towers on each side with 3 inhibitors and 1 nexus on each side of the 2 teams.
The goal is to destroy the towers in the lanes until you come to the inhibitors, then you want to
destroy the 2 towers that are in the way of the nexus crystal to be able to end the game.

All that you have to do while getting objectives like one of the 5 dragons until one team has 4 dragons,
Rift Heralds or Baron Nashor.

You can see everything on the map to the left.
